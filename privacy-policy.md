# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Headphone Alarm ("the App") respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
All alarm processing happens locally on your device.

## Bluetooth Access

The app checks whether headphones are connected through Bluetooth to ensure alarms play privately in headphones.
No Bluetooth data is stored or transmitted.

## Analytics

The app does not use analytics services.
## Third-Party Services

The app does not share data with any third parties.

# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Headphone Alarm ("the App") respects your privacy.

## Data Collection by the App

The App itself does not require account registration and does not directly collect or store personal data on its own servers.
Alarm processing and headphone connection checks are primarily handled on your device.

## Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain data for purposes such as ad delivery, analytics, fraud prevention, and performance monitoring.

This data may include, depending on usage and configuration:
- Device identifiers
- Advertising data
- IP address
- Approximate location
- Product interaction data
- Diagnostics and performance data

For more information, please refer to Google's policies:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Bluetooth Access

The App may check whether headphones are connected through Bluetooth in order to support private alarm playback.
This information is not stored by the App on its own servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services including Google AdMob, which may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
